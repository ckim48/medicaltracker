Project Overview

Discover our specially tailored mobile application designed to revolutionize sleep monitoring. Utilizing advanced sensors in mobile devices, our app collects and analyzes sleep data, alerting supervisors of health-threatening issues.

Goal

Initially designed to cater to elderly individuals living independently with disruptive sleep disorders, this application gathers, analyzes, and visually represents sleep data. The aim is to promptly identify abnormal sleeping movements or behaviors in real-time, for ultimately enhancing intervention strategies.

Next Steps
